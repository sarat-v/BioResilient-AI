## What this step did

Nucleotide conservation layer (DNA-level, supplementary to protein analysis). For each gene × species pair, downloads the genome GFF3 annotation and genomic FASTA from NCBI, extracts three sequence windows: CDS (coding sequence), promoter (5 kb upstream of TSS), and downstream (2 kb after gene end). Aligns each resilient species against human using minimap2 -x asm5 (asm-to-asm preset). Computes conservation_score = (pct_identity × alignment_length) / region_length. For promoter regions, counts regulatory_divergence_count (mutations shared by ≥3 resilient species, absent in human + controls) and regulatory_convergence_count (same mutation in ≥2 distinct evolutionary lineage clusters — e.g. whale + bat but not controls). This captures regulatory evolution that protein alignments cannot detect.
